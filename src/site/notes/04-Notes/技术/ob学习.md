Certainly, here are some blogs that provide insights on how to schedule PhD life:

  

1. [Start the Semester off Right: Make a Weekly Template](http://getalifephd.blogspot.com/2013/08/start-semester-off-right-make-weekly.html): This blog post discusses creating a weekly schedule that includes your fixed appointments for the semester and also time for other necessary tasks.
    
2. [My PhD schedule](https://www.evalantsoght.com/2014/03/my-phd-schedule.html): Here the author shares her personal weekly schedule during her PhD, which includes time for errands, self-study, blogging, reading and relaxation.
    
3. [PhD student lifestyle: 6 Steps to create the perfect routine](https://researcher.life/blog/article/phd-student-lifestyle-6-steps-to-create-the-perfect-routine/): This blog outlines six steps to create the perfect routine during your PhD journey, which includes tips on scheduling and work structuring.
    
4. [Thoughts on Productivity | Day in the Life of a PhD Student](http://ellayvonne.com/blog/on-productivity-day-in-the-life-phd-student): The author of this blog shares her daily schedule from Monday through Friday, reflecting on her productivity as a PhD student.
    
5. [How Time Blocking and Time Tracking Can Keep You on Track While Working from Home](http://getalifephd.blogspot.com/2022/09/how-time-blocking-and-time-tracking-can.html): This blog post discusses how time blocking and time tracking can help you stay on track while working from home.
    

  

You might find different approaches in each of these blogs. It's important to find a method that works best for you, keeping in mind that your PhD journey is unique.
